Google Nexus Galaxy Nexus Google Nexus is a line of mobile devices using the Android operating system produced by Google in cooperation with hardware manufacturer Devices in the Nexus series do not have carrier or manufacturer modifications to Android and have an unlockable bootloader to allow further development and end-user modificatio Nexus devices are the first Android devices to receive updates to the operating syste The November 2011 Galaxy Nexus is one of the few phones recommended by the Android Open Source Project for Android software development As of November 2012 [update] the
